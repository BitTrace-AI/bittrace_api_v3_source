# Patent Pending Notice

## Recommended Wording

Use restrained factual wording only:

> Patent Pending. Certain core BitTrace symbolic packed-bit classification
> methods and systems are the subject of one or more pending U.S. patent
> applications.

## Placement

Reasonable placement options:

- this repository root notice file
- the licensing section of `README.md`
- `docs/HANDBOOK.md`
- release-bundle cover material

## Rules

When using the notice:

- keep the wording factual
- do not claim broader patent scope than the wording supports
- do not imply that the notice changes the rights granted by `LICENSE.md`
- do not use the notice as legal advice

## Avoid These Claims

Do not say:

- that all BitTrace methods are patented
- that use is forbidden merely because a patent application is pending
- that the notice itself changes evaluation or commercial rights
- that the pending applications cover all packed-bit classification systems

The patent notice is informational only. Rights are governed by `LICENSE.md` and
any separate written commercial agreement.
